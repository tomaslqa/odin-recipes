Muffins

Description

Basic muffin recipe

Ingredients:

  • 115 g of butter
  • 2 eggs (large)
  • 160 ml of milk
  • 130 g of sugar
  • 1 vanilla sugar
  • 260 g of plain flour
  • 1 baking powder
  • pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees.
  2. Mix eggs, milk and vanilla sugar/vanilla extract.
  3. In another bowl, mix sugar, flour, baking powder and salt.
  4. Melt the butter.
  5. Slowly mix the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients, along with the melted butter, using a spatula.
  6. Pour mixture in baking tray.
  7. Bake for about 20 minutes.
  8. Leave to cool for 10 minutes.
