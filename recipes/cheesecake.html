Cheesecake

Description

Best cheesecake I have ever made

Ingredients:

  • 200 g of Petit Beure biscuits
  • 5 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 6 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 120 g of butter
  • 500 g of cream cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 vanilla sugar
  • 2 sour creams (20% fat) 360 g

Steps:

  1. Line the bottom of a round cake pan with greaseproof paper and sprinkle 2 tablespoons of brown sugar on it.
  2. Preheat the oven to 250C.
  3. Grind or crush 200 g of Petit Beure biscuits and mix with 3 tablespoons of brown sugar and 120 g of pre-melted butter, mix everything well with your hands.
  4. Put the mixture in the mold and press so that it is evenly distributed everywhere.
  5. Put in the oven for exactly 4 minutes.
  6. While it is baking, prepare the yellow cream: Mix 500 g of fresh cheese, the juice of 1 lemon, 2 whole eggs, 3 tablespoons of regular sugar and 1 vanilla sugar.
  7. Take the baked biscuits out after 4 minutes and reduce the oven to 170C.
  8. Pour the yellow cream immediately over the hot biscuits without any waiting and return immediately to the oven to bake for exactly 20 minutes.
  9. When it's baking, prepare the white cream: Mix 2 sour creams, 3 spoons of regular sugar and 1 vanilla sugar.
  10. When the yellow cream is baked, turn off the oven, pour the white cream over it and put it in the turned off oven for another 5 minutes.
  11. The cake must be kept in the fridge for 4-5 hours before consumption.
